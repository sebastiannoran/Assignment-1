Air Quality Impact

On June 2023, Canadian wildfires blanketed a majority portion of the Northeastern United States with smoke. Pollutants traveled to major urban centers and caused air quality to reach harzdous levels. At its peak air quality in NYC reached a hazadous air quality index (AQI) of 400. This metric measures the amount of polluation in the air for a certain time. Under normal circumstances, a reading of 100-200 would trigger a health warning for sensitive groups.
